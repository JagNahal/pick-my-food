About Pick My Food

Pick My Food is a web application designed to help users discover their next favorite restaurant. By simply entering your city and choosing a preferred cuisine, our application will provide a personalized recommendation for a fantastic dining experience.

Our goal is to connect food lovers with amazing restaurants and to make the process of discovering new places to eat enjoyable and straightforward. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a fine dining experience, Pick My Food has you covered.
